Unit 1: MS Word

MS Word basics: Working with documents, formatting tools, page setup, tables, graphics, document management, and essential editing features.

Key Topics:

  • Introduction to MS Word
  • Working with Documents (Open, Save, Edit, Insert, Delete, Copy-Paste)
  • Document Formatting (Fonts, Paragraphs, Alignment, Bullets, Numbering)
  • Page Formatting (Margins, Layout, Columns, Header & Footer)
  • Breaks & Sections (Page, Column, Line Breaks, Wrapping, Frames)
  • Tables (Create, Format, Merge, Split, Sort, Formulas)
  • Tools & Features (Spell Check, Mail Merge, Templates)
  • Graphics & Drawing Tools (ClipArt, Pictures, Symbols)
  • Document Management (TOC, Index, Page Numbers, Footnotes)
  • Security & Printing (Digital Signature, Track Changes, Shortcuts)
