Unit 1: Introduction to Digital Electronics

Introduction to Digital Electronics: Number System, Boolean Algebra, Minimization of Boolean Expressions using K-Map; Logic Gates and implementation of logic functions; Combinational and Sequential Circuits.

Key Topics:

  • Number System: Binary, Octal, Decimal, Hexadecimal
  • Boolean Algebra
  • K-Map - Minimization of Boolean Expressions
  • Logic Gates & Implementation of Logic Functions
  • Combinational Circuits
  • Adders & Subtractors
  • Multiplexer & De-Multiplexer
  • Decoder
  • Sequential Circuits
  • Flip-Flops: Types & Excitation Table
  • Registers - Introduction & Classification
  • Counters - Synchronous & Asynchronous
